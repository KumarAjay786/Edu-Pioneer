Who We Are

Education Pioneer is India's premium educational consulting platform that offers an extensive range of information to one and all. Based in Delhi, Education Pioneer has a global approach in offering education solutions with a gamut of information that falls between the academics domain. Being an online education solution provider, Education Pioneer is progressively becoming an individual platform to gain knowledge about academic profiles for students of all age groups. We are a major provider of education consultancy in India, aiming at integrating academic options, solutions, research, study resources, organizations - whether in India or abroad, at one place! Our expertise lies not just in Indian education situation, but we are also well-versed with education abroad and their prerequisites, i.e. if you wish to study outside India. Our knowledge and expertise is reflected in the information we share with our clients through our website and in person.

Our Mission

The chief aim of Education Pioneer is to suffice the lack of information related to study and education in India. When it comes to education, parents as well as students face a lot of difficulty in getting accurate and refined information from valid sources. This is where Education Pioneer aims to stand as the leading solution provider. Not only does Education Pioneer brings about the best of all the available educational options in India, it also acts as a true mentor in helping you know more about the various academic courses like selection of school/college/university, vetting the ideal syllabus for courses and international education protocols are a few. In conclusion, Education Pioneer aims at becoming a global education hub that establishes itself as an outright one-stop platform for all education related queries and solutions.

Our Vision

Education in India has rapidly improved over the years, with a significant progress to make education accessible for all.
